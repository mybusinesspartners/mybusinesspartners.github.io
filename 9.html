Department of Finance Business Centers provide a variety of services for parking and camera violations, property taxes, and other programs.

At a center, you can:

Pay parking and camera violations, towing and booting fees, property taxes and related charges Have hearings on parking and camera violations that are less than 1 year old Request tax or registration clearances, proof of satisfaction, or release documents Get or submit program forms, applications, and guides Get general assistance and program information

All centers are open Monday through Friday, 8:30 AM to 4:30 PM.

Schedule an Appointment

You can schedule an appointment to ensure social distancing at DOF Business Centers. Walk-in customers are still accepted.

You can schedule appointments for:

Booted/towed vehicles and parking payment plans Commercial Motor Vehicle Tax (CMVT) Making payments Property tax lien sale and payment agreements Other customer services Cash bail (Only available at Manhattan Business Center): Abandonment letter received Form/document drop-off Refund check pickup Court & Trust: Form/document drop-off Withdrawal check pickup In-Person Hearing for Parking Tickets or Camera Violations. Appointments are NOT available for brokers/representatives that are registered in Fleet/Non-Fleet (Commercial Adjudications) or for appeals

Schedule an appointment.

Payment Methods

You can pay using:

Cash Personal or certified check * in U.S. currency payable to the “NYC Department of Finance” Money order in U.S. currency payable to the “NYC Department of Finance” Credit card (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover, or foreign cards with CVVs) Debit card (Visa, MasterCard, or American Express logo) Pre-paid card or gift card (Visa, MasterCard, or American Express logo)

A 2% service fee is added for credit, debit, and pre-paid or gift card payments. There s no fee for payments by cash, check, or money order.

* Personal checks are not accepted for payment if a vehicle has been booted or towed.
